What are the two statements embodying the University's Catholic identity?
Answer: "Education with a Mission" and "Witness to the Word."
According to the text, what does USC aim for in the holistic formation of individuals?
Answer: USC aims at enlightening the mind, honing skills, and positively transforming character and behavior.
How are Carolinians described in society according to the text?
Answer: Carolinians are described as the "light," "salt," and "yeast" of the Gospel.
What qualities do graduates of USC possess according to the text?
Answer: Graduates of USC are professionally competent and skilled, have noble character, and are dedicated to social transformation.
What does the University of San Carlos envision for the world?
Answer: The University of San Carlos envisions a world where the darkness of sin and the night of unbelief vanish before the light of the Word and the Spirit of grace.
How does the text describe the society envisioned by the University of San Carlos?
Answer: The society envisioned by the University of San Carlos consists of citizens who are competent, noble in character, and community-oriented.
What is the mission of the University of San Carlos according to the text?
Answer: The mission of the University of San Carlos is to develop competent and socially responsible professionals and lifelong learners.
What are the principles that the University of San Carlos embodies?
Answer: The University of San Carlos embodies the principles of academic discipline of San Carlos Borromeo and the missionary charism of the Society of the Divine Word (SVD).
What are the academic core processes emphasized by the University of San Carlos?
Answer: Teaching-learning, research, and community extension service.
What kind of academic programs does the University of San Carlos aim to provide?
Answer: The University of San Carlos aims to provide timely, relevant, and transformative academic programs responsive to the needs of the local, national, and global communities.
What are the core values of the University of San Carlos?
Answer: Integrity, Excellence, Commitment, Social responsibility, Evangelization, and Leadership.
How are Carolinians expected to reflect integrity in their personal and professional lives?
Answer: Carolinians are expected to reflect the ideals of the Catholic university as an academic community that assists in the protection and advancement of human dignity and cultural heritage.
What is the goal of the University of San Carlos, according to the text?
Answer: The ultimate goal of the University of San Carlos is the transformation of communities through the provision of quality basic and higher education.
How are Carolinians encouraged to address prevailing social realities in the country?
Answer: Carolinians are encouraged to provide themselves and the students an understanding of and effective tools for addressing the prevailing social realities in the country.
What is the role of Carolinians in evangelization according to the text?
Answer: Carolinians shall seek to understand the values and mores of local cultures and enrich them through gospel values and the teachings of the Church.
How are Carolinians expected to exhibit leadership?
Answer: Carolinians, not only in positions of authority but also in their own personal capacity, shall strive to set the conditions for reflection and learning on their respective educational tasks.
What are the characteristics of a Carolinian in terms of integrity?
Answer: Carolinians shall reflect in their personal and professional life the ideals of the Catholic university.
How should Carolinians strive in terms of excellence?
